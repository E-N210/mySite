About the Project

“Message in AR Bottle” is a Geolocation-based AR experience. Other users might have placed some messages around you, start the experience and look for them!

You can also add your own messages and other users will be able to view them in the place you decided.

All Messages are linked to a physical GPS position and are displayed in the real world according to those coordinates. The AR scene allows you to explore the contents while moving in the real world, looking for hidden messages.

Unseen Indicators

Unseen Indicators is one my first Experiments with augmented reality! Attach the sticker and see Lichens grow on every surface!

Look the preview video.

About the Project

During my erasmus at FHP-Potsdam i had the chance explore new narrations about woods and nature. With my team mate Lukas we dacided to work on lichens, showing their growth.

He created the 3d models of lichens using generative algorithems, while i paced them in the real world using Vuforia and their Marker-Based tracking feature.

This was the very project that made me fall in love with AR!

AudioScape

AudioScape is aseries of sound-reactive sketches realized with P5.js press the button snd turn every sound into awesome visuals!

About the Project

I enjoy the idea of sinesthesia and creating connection between senses. That's what lies behind AudioScape.

A series of sketches that turns the input from your microphone (captured using p5.sound.js) to turn it into visual arts. The sketches are rsponsive and every frame is generated based on your voice. We can use this system to explore a new world of interactive visual arts, working to create the right visual effect for every music
